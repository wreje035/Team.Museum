HUTTON THEATRE

This multipurpose theatre is large and versatile with removable furniture.

Suitable for:

  • Balls
  • Christmas parties
  • Cocktail functions
  • Conferences
  • Lectures

KAKAPO & HUIA MEETING ROOMS

This multipurpose space is excellent both as a stand-alone seminar room and as a breakout room for the Hutton Theatre. It can be divided by a moveable wall, which can be retracted to create one larger space.

ADMISSION
Entry to the Museum and the galleries is free. This does not include Tūhura, which incorporates the Otago Community Trust Science Centre, the Perpetual Guardian Planetarium or the Beautiful Science gallery. Prices are below.

ACCESSIBILITY

Galleries and exhibitions are accessible by wheelchair and pram. If you need our free wheelchair, just ask at the Info Desk. The Museum offers baby changing facilities. There is a play area next to the Café.
