HUTTON THEATRE

This multipurpose theatre is large and versatile with removable furniture. Suitable for: Balls; Christmas parties; cocktail functions; conferences; lectures; presentations; receptions; weddings.
Visit the museum site for more info
×

KAKAPO & HUIA MEETING ROOMS

This multipurpose space is excellent both as a stand-alone seminar room and as a breakout room for the Hutton Theatre. It can be divided by a moveable wall, which can be retracted to create one larger space. Suitable for: Conference breakout sessions; lectures; presentations; seminars. Capacity: Banquet or cabaret: 24 Theatre: 50 Social function: 50 Boardroom or classroom: 24

ACCESSIBILITY

Galleries and exhibitions are accessible by wheelchair and pram. If you need our free wheelchair, just ask at the Info Desk. The Museum offers baby changing facilities. There is a play area next to the Café.
