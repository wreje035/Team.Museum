FRIENDS ROOM

Join The Friends In 1926, the Association of Friends of the Otago Museum met for the first time. It was the brainchild of former Otago Museum Director H D Skinner; its first chairman was benefactor Willi Fels. This group of diverse, yet like-minded people came together with a singular purpose: to support the Museum.
